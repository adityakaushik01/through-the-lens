## ✨ Overview

**Through The Lens** is a modern digital photography exhibition platform designed to showcase a photographer's work in a visually immersive environment.

Instead of presenting photographs in a traditional grid layout, the application recreates the feeling of walking through a curated art gallery using modern web technologies and smooth 3D interactions.

## 🎨 Vision

Photography is more than images—it is storytelling through light, perspective, and emotion.

Through The Lens aims to bridge photography and digital experiences by creating an online exhibition space where visitors don't just view photographs—they experience them.
